System requirements

What you'll need

Minimum

  • Windows 10 64-bit (build 19041+) or Windows 11
  • OpenGL 4.3-capable GPU (anything from the last ~10 years)
  • 8 GB RAM
  • 2 GB free disk space

Recommended for live performance

  • Windows 11
  • NVIDIA RTX-class or AMD RX 6000-class GPU
  • 16+ GB RAM
  • SSD with at least 10 GB free for cache and recordings
  • Dedicated GPU (no integrated graphics for stage use)

macOS and Linux builds aren't on the roadmap yet — Windows-only for the 1.0 release.
