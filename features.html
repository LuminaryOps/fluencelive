Features

Everything you'd build a rig out of.

Real-time rendering, layered composition, audio reactivity, hardware mapping, recording — engineered as one system, not glued together.

Rendering

OpenGL pipeline, frame-perfect timing.

Every layer composites on the GPU. Fluence isolates the audio thread, watches GPU resource lifetimes, and keeps allocations out of the per-frame path — so 60fps stays 60fps when the room is full and you're sweating the build-up.

Sources

Shaders, video, generators — one browser.

Built-in patterns, procedurals, raymarched 3D, fractals, particle systems, and the entire ISF library are searchable from a single browser. Drop a GLSL or ISF file into the shaders folder and it shows up there too.

Composition

Layers, slots, columns, and an arrangement timeline.

Stack layers, fill slots with clips, group layers into routes. Switch between deck-style triggering and an arrangement timeline you can scrub. Familiar VJ flow, modern internals — copy/paste, undo/redo, save/load all work the way you'd expect.

Effects

Per-clip, per-layer, or composition-wide.

Drop effects on a single clip, on a whole layer, or across the entire composition. Every parameter is mappable, automatable, and persisted with your project.

Audio reactivity

Beat detection, FFT, and loopback.

Capture the system audio output or a dedicated input. Tap BPM, pull frequency bands, and route them into uniforms or effect parameters with a Linear transform — visuals that follow the room, not the click track.

Hardware control

Keyboard, MIDI, OSC — one mapping store.

Edit Keyboard / MIDI / OSC modes capture bindings in two clicks. Map clips, layers, transport, BPM tap, effect knobs — anywhere a parameter exists. One source can fan out to many targets, so a single key or note can launch a whole scene.

Output

Multi-screen, screen capture, recording.

Send to one display, several, or a capture card. Capture another window or a region of your desktop and use it as a live source. Record straight to disk in a single click.

That's the tour.
